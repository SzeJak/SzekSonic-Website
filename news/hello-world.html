Welcome to SzekSonic

We are a young and passionate team from Prague developing the next generation of drones. This site is the start of how we'll share what we're building, what we're learning, and where we're going.

SzekSonic exists to answer one question: how do you stop fast, low-cost, and harder-to-engage threats before they reach their target? Everything we do — from the airframes we design to the software we write — is in service of that question.
