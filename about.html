// About

A Czech team with one question.

How do you stop fast, low-cost, and harder-to-engage threats before they reach their target? That's the only question we work on.

// Who we are

Young, passionate, focused.

SzekSonic s.r.o. is an early-stage defense technology company headquartered in Hluk, Czech Republic. We're a small team of engineers, operators, and builders united by the conviction that Europe needs sovereign capability in unmanned defense — and that capability has to be fast, scalable, and affordable.

We design our architecture, our hardware and build our prototypes inside the European Union. Our supply chain reflects that commitment: Made in EU is not a marketing line for us, it's an engineering constraint we hold ourselves to.

// Mission

Speed and precision, at scale.

The threat landscape has changed. Cheap, fast, hard-to-engage systems can overwhelm traditional defenses. SzekSonic exists to give defenders solutions that match the speed and economics of those threats — not solutions that cost ten times more than what they intercept.
